What does basic SEO Pack means?

Our Basic SEO Pack is a fundamental search engine optimization (SEO) service designed to enhance the visibility of your website on search engines like Google. It includes essential SEO elements such as keyword research, on-page optimization, meta tag optimization, and sitemap submission. With the Basic SEO Pack, we work to ensure that your website is well-optimized for search engines, making it easier for potential customers to find you online.

It's important to note that the Basic SEO Pack provides a strong foundation for SEO but may not cover advanced optimization strategies or ongoing SEO efforts. Depending on your goals and competition, you may consider upgrading to a more comprehensive SEO plan to achieve even greater visibility and results in search engine rankings.

How do refunds work?

We understand that circumstances can change, and we aim to provide a fair and transparent refund policy. Refunds are handled in accordance with the terms outlined in your project proposal or agreement. Here's an overview of how refunds typically work:

  • Initial Deposit: If you've made an initial deposit to secure your project, the refund policy for that deposit will be specified in your agreement. It may be refundable within a certain timeframe or under specific conditions.
  • Project Progress: Refunds may be available on a pro-rated basis if your project has not reached a certain stage of development. This stage will be defined in your agreement.
  • Scope Changes: If there are changes to the project scope, the impact on project costs and refunds will be discussed and agreed upon in writing.
  • Transparency: We are committed to clear and open communication regarding refunds. We'll work closely with you to ensure you understand the refund process and its implications for your project.

It's important to review the refund terms and conditions in your project agreement and reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns. Our goal is to provide a fair and equitable resolution in line with the terms agreed upon in your contract.
